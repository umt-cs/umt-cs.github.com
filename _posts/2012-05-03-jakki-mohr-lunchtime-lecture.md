[Jakki Mohr](http://www.business.umt.edu/FacultyStaff/JakkiMohr.aspx), Regents
Professor of Marketing at UM's business school, is giving a talk on high-tech
marketing tomorrow at noon in SS 362.

Her talk is "Marketing and Commercialization Strategies for Information
Technology: Keys to Success" and will cover:

- How marketing complements IT development for successful deployment
- What it means to understand "the user experience" and why that is critical
  for success
- Key tools to develop a commercialization strategy for your software
  developments

Also, don't forget about the department picnic at 4 PM in Bonner Park!
